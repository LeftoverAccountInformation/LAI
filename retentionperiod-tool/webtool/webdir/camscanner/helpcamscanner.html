How can I delete my account?

If you think that you will no longer use CS, you can apply for a permanent deletion of the CS account.

Please note you will not be able to reactivate the account or restore the data in your CS account after you delete it. Your purchased items (such as fax credits, C-Points, etc.) can't be restored either.

If you have subscribed to the premium service, please cancel the service in your Apple or Google account first.

If you want to permanently delete the CS account and clear all synchronized data, after exporting the documents, please delete all documents in the account and send the Delete account request to support@intsig.com

The account will be deleted in 14 days after we receive your email.
