# One app to save everything

Collect by WeTransfer is the best way to organize your ideas. Save content
from across your apps and bring it together for your friends, your team, or
just for yourself.

## Save anything from anywhere at any time

Collect, keep and combine ideas from all of your apps – songs, pictures,
videos, links, articles and anything else you can think of.

![Collect and keep your items in one place](./Collect by
WeTransfer_files/collect.png)

## Show off your stuff in style

Say goodbye to confusing notes and forgotten folders of screenshots. Organize
your ideas as beautiful boards that are perfect for visual thinkers.

![Grow your idea with friends](./Collect by WeTransfer_files/friends.png)

## Grow your ideas with others

Invite anyone to add to your board so you can collaborate and create together.
Then share it with a simple link to send your ideas out into the world.

![Stay in the loop](./Collect by WeTransfer_files/previews.png)

## Stay in your flow

On your phone, on the go, it doesn’t matter. Save things for later with a
couple of clicks and stay focussed on what you’re doing.

![Get it on iOS and Android](./Collect by WeTransfer_files/phones.png)
