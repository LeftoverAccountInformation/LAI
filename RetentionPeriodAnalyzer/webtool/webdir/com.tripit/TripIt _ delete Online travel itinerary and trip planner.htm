Warning! By closing your account, all trip information, preferences, connections, and all other account data will be permanently deleted with no ability to recover the data. We highly recommend you not close your account unless you are absolutely certain that you do not need any of the information contained in it.

Why are you closing?

  • If you are closing this account because you are trying to add the email address to another TripIt account you can instead merge your account.
  • If you would rather keep your TripIt account open, but would like to receive less email from TripIt, you can modify your email preferences on the Email Settings page.

Note: Even after you've closed your account, it's possible that you could receive email from people you know inviting you to connect or view itineraries on TripIt. If you are confident that you never want to be contacted through TripIt again, you can add your email address to our "do not contact" list".

Do Not Contact List

Adding an email address to our "do not contact" list means:
  • You will never receive emails through TripIt, even if someone you know invites you to view a trip or connect
  • You cannot use this email address to register for TripIt in the future
  • You cannot add this email address to any other TripIt account

Please select the email addresses that you would like added to our "do not contact" list.
